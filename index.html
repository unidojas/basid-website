App Features

Services That We Offer To The Users

SMS Notification

SMS notification informs the receiver about an event such as an update on crisis or alert services.

Geotagging

Geotagging-based management contributes information regarding solid waste issues which will then be received by the authorities.

Website Information

A web application is important for citizen science projects because of the capability to monitor activities in real-time, it allows communication between the general public and authorities which helps alleviate solid waste issues.

Android OS

The Android OS is an open source operating system primarily used in mobile devices.

Data Visualization

Data visualizations contain contextual information, making the information more straightforward for people to process and analyze.

Image Filtering (Optional)

Image processing refers to the process of recognizing, making algorithmic enhancements and manipulating a digital image.

Frequently Asked Questions

What Can We Help You Find?

  • What is BASID Application?

    BASID is an application that will focus on monitoring solid waste that helps the citizen science community of Cagayan de Oro City in making relevant policies and programs to combat solid waste issues. The limitations of this study are android-based devices, the need of internet connection to use the mobile app and the prospect client to use the web application for monitoring.

  • Who are behind the project BASID?

    The project BASID Application was developed by University of Science and Technology of Southern Philipines (USTP-CDO) students.

  • How do I contact the BASID Team?

    The BASID Team can be reached at basid@gmail.com. You may also visit us at USTP-CDO campus.
